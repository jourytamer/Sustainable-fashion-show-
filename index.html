Sustainable Fashion Show 2025

Sustainable fashion is all about creating clothing in a way that protects the environment and supports ethical labor practices. It focuses on using eco-friendly materials, reducing waste, and promoting recycling. This fashion show will showcase designs made from organic fabrics, recycled materials, and innovative production techniques that help reduce the fashion industry's carbon footprint.

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) plays a vital role in sustainable fashion by enabling brands to track their supply chains, reduce waste through digital design tools, and promote ethical fashion through online platforms. With advancements in AI, blockchain, and e-commerce, ICT helps create transparency and encourages consumers to make environmentally friendly fashion choices.
